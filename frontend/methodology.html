Research-Driven Methodology

Use this structure to execute your major project from problem framing to production deployment.

  1. Baseline Benchmarking: train each model on a stable split and record Accuracy, F1, ROC-AUC, PR-AUC, calibration error, and inference latency.
  2. Controlled Shift Injection: introduce gradual drift, abrupt concept shift, noise corruption, and class imbalance independently and then jointly.
  3. Temporal Evaluation: evaluate at each time step to build degradation curves, failure points, and warning thresholds.
  4. Root-Cause Diagnostics: compare SHAP/feature importance drift, calibration decay, and subgroup performance gaps.
  5. Mitigation Loop: trigger retraining, active learning refresh, and threshold tuning, then compare recovery quality vs baseline.
